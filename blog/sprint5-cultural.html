Core Reflection

What are three new things you have learned about yourself and your ego as a result of the core learning?

I found mindfulness practice difficult as my mind just is just actively thinking all the time. Doing the coding exercises, I learned that I can still focus and be on a state of flow for prolonged periods of time. Doing the listening and looping exercise, I found out that it is hard for me to speak for 2 minutes straight talking about my experiences and feelings.

What are the role of values, empathy and self awareness in learning and programming?

Learning of these three enables one to accept his/her strengths and weaknesses and allows one to determine the learning method most suited for him/her.

What has surprised you the most about the core learning?

It surprised me to know that that reflecting is an important aspect of learning.

What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

Writing my own thoughts and publishing it in a blog is the most challenging for me.

Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development bootcamp course?

Because in the bootcamp and consequently in actual practice we will be facing difficulties. Thus we need to learn to be resilient and have a framework for effective problem solving.

Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practising programming instead? Justify your answer.

No, the things we learned on the core topics are related to human skills which are just as vital as technical skills if we want to make it in the industry.
